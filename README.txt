assets by ivoryred on itch.io
https://ivoryred.itch.io/pixel-poker-cards?download
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
controls:
 - left click: pick up card / deselect selection
 - right click: flip card
 - ctrl + left click hold: select cards completely in green area 
 - f: flip selected cards
 - d: make a deck out of selected cards
 - s: shuffle selected cards unorganized
 - hold d + s: shuffle cards and make them a deck (use repeatedly to 	shuffle multiple times
 - hold r: move all cards towards mouse
 - hold c: move all selected cards towards mouse
